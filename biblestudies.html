Daily Bible Study

Today’s Study: John 3:16

This passage speaks about God's love for the world and the gift of eternal life through belief in Jesus Christ.

  • Context: This verse is often quoted in a discussion of salvation and God's love.
  • Key Verse: "For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life."
  • Reflection: How does this verse impact your understanding of God’s love?
  • Application: Consider how you can share this message of love with others today.

Next Study: Philippians 4:6-7

This passage encourages us not to be anxious but to pray and find peace in God’s presence.

  • Context: Paul's letter offers guidance on how to live joyfully in Christ.
  • Key Verse: "Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God."
  • Reflection: Think about a situation you're anxious about and how prayer can change your perspective.
  • Application: Make it a priority to bring your worries to God in prayer this week.
